ChatGPT

OpenAI

General-purpose all-rounder. Writing, brainstorming, client emails, quick answers. Your reliable, widely available go-to for most situations.

Gemini

Claude

The Next Wave

Agentic AI

AI that doesn't just answer — it does things for you. Runs on your computer, connected to your apps, files, and messages.

“Build me a presentation”

Describe what you want. The agent has its own computer with PowerPoint. It builds and sends the file back to you.

“Book me a table for Friday”

The agent calls the restaurant using a human-sounding voice. Confirms the booking and updates your diary.

“Clear my inbox”

Reads and categorises your emails. Drafts replies, flags urgent items. All from a message on your phone.
